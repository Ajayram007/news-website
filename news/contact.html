Help and FAQs

In this help site we have gathered together our most frequently asked questions asked by our audience. You can use the search bar above to search our FAQs. Top Tip: Try searching with a keyword, like the name of the programme or service you have a question about.

If any of our FAQs don't answer your question see How can I ask a question about a BBC programme or service? or How can I contact the BBC?

Comments

We appreciate all comments, appreciative or otherwise, as it’s helpful to know what you think. We will circulate your comment the next morning for all staff to see.

If you would like to understand how we collect and use personal data, please refer to our privacy notice.

Complaints

These pages have information about how to complain to the BBC, with links to the BBC’s Complaints Framework, the BBC’s regulator Ofcom and regular reports about complaints.
